Paper-inspired EWAS SVA demo

This page summarizes the additional use case added to the repo: a synthetic multi-cohort methylation EWAS designed to resemble the modeling pattern of a published Alzheimer's disease meta-analysis. It is inspiration, not reproduction.

What the notebook demonstrates

  • continuous Braak-stage-like burden score
  • multiple cohorts and brain-region style tissues
  • measured covariates plus hidden technical and biological variation
  • surrogate variable estimation
  • tuning the number of SVs using lambda control
